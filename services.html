Influencer Campaign – Social Media (Business)

Contact us at authentic.influence@gmail.com for pricing.

Authentic Influence will put together a carefully selected team of influencers that will represent your business. These influencers will be given a background on your business and product/service that they will be promoting. We ensure that all influencers are genuine users/supporters of the business they are matched with to ensure the authenticity of the partnership.

Authentic Influence will be responsible for the following campaign activities

  • Casting of influencers, contract negotiation, management of posts, approvals of creative & content, collecting all content and sending to the business to reuse on their own platforms, payment of influencers and providing an analytical report of the campaign.

Add-on Services
